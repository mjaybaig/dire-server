Auto-Recognize Farming Equipment

With the help of Artificial Intelligence, we have put together a feature that enables you to simply scan a picture of your equipment, and we'll identify it for you and provide safety information regarding that machine!

Detailed Climate Report

We fetch up-to-date weather, UV and air quality data as well as forecasts, and deliver them to you so you can plan you day safely. We also provide advice tailored to the type of climate you may be experiencing at the moment

Nearby Hospitals

We fetch contact details for hospitals that are near your location incase you need any medical assistance. With the tap of a button you can immediately get directions to your chosen hospital!
